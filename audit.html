Executive Summary

This accessibilty report was ran on my website that was created for my digital publishing class. The purpose was to inform me of where my webpage might be lacking in accessibility, and to inform others of the corrections that I made.

Recording of Evaluation Specifics

I added a disclosure about my images linking to another page, added regions, and programmed english as the main language. The disclosure makes my page more accessible for people who accidentally click and are not prepared to go to another page. I added regions and declared a language to make my page more accessible for people who need technology assistance.
